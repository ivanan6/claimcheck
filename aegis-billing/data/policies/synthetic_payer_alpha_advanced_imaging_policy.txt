SYNTHETIC PAYER ALPHA
Advanced Diagnostic Imaging Coverage Policy
Policy ID: SPA-IMG-2026-01
Effective date: 2026-01-01

Important notice:
This is a synthetic policy created for the AegisBilling.ai demo. It does not
represent a real payer policy and does not copy proprietary procedure code
descriptions.

Covered service group:
- PROC-IMG-MRI-BRAIN: advanced brain imaging study
- PROC-IMG-MRI-KNEE: advanced knee imaging study
- PROC-IMG-CT-ABD: advanced abdominal imaging study

Medical necessity requirements:
1. The diagnosis and physician note must support the requested imaging study.
2. The note should describe symptoms, duration, physical examination findings,
   prior conservative treatment when applicable, and the clinical question to be
   answered by imaging.
3. Imaging performed only for screening or patient preference is not covered.

Prior authorization requirements:
Every claim package for PROC-IMG-MRI-BRAIN, PROC-IMG-MRI-KNEE, or
PROC-IMG-CT-ABD must include a prior authorization approval identifier unless the
service was provided during an emergency encounter.

Required documents:
- Physician imaging order
- Clinical note supporting medical necessity
- Prior authorization approval document or emergency exception statement

Recent duplicate imaging rule:
If the same advanced imaging service was performed within the last 180 days, the
claim package must include a repeat-imaging justification. The justification must
explain the change in symptoms, failed treatment, new neurological findings,
trauma, surgical planning need, or other clinical reason for repeating the study.

Denial rules:
- Claims without prior authorization are denied unless an emergency exception is
  documented.
- Repeat advanced imaging within 180 days without justification is denied.
- Claims without the physician imaging order are denied as incomplete.

Submission checklist for PROC-IMG-MRI-BRAIN:
- Diagnosis supports neurological or brain-related indication
- Physician imaging order attached
- Clinical note attached
- Prior authorization approval attached
- Repeat-imaging justification attached if same imaging was done within 180 days
