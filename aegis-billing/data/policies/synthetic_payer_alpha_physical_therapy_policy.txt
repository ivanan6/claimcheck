SYNTHETIC PAYER ALPHA
Rehabilitation Services Coverage Policy
Policy ID: SPA-REHAB-2026-01
Effective date: 2026-01-01

Important notice:
This is a synthetic policy created for the AegisBilling.ai demo. It does not
represent a real payer policy and does not copy proprietary procedure code
descriptions.

Covered service group:
- PROC-PT-THEREX: supervised therapeutic exercise and rehabilitation session
- PROC-PT-MANUAL: supervised manual therapy session
- PROC-PT-REEDU: supervised neuromuscular re-education session

Medical necessity requirements:
1. The submitted diagnosis must describe an injury, postoperative recovery,
   mobility limitation, pain, weakness, or functional impairment that is
   consistent with rehabilitation services.
2. The physician note must describe the functional limitation and expected
   rehabilitation goal.
3. The planned therapy must be expected to improve function, range of motion,
   strength, gait, balance, or activities of daily living.

Required documents for initial therapy billing:
Every claim package for PROC-PT-THEREX, PROC-PT-MANUAL, or PROC-PT-REEDU must
include all of the following:
- Physician referral or signed order for therapy
- Initial physical therapy evaluation
- Written plan of care with frequency, duration, measurable goals, and treatment
  modalities

Additional required documents:
If billing more than four therapy sessions in one claim package, the package
must also include a progress report showing response to treatment and remaining
functional limitations.

Denial rules:
- Claims without a physician referral or signed order are denied as incomplete.
- Claims without an initial physical therapy evaluation are denied as incomplete.
- Claims without a written plan of care are denied as insufficient documentation.
- Claims for more than four sessions without a progress report are denied as
  insufficient progress documentation.
- A claim may be medically reasonable but still denied if required attachments
  are missing.

Submission checklist for PROC-PT-THEREX:
- Diagnosis supports rehabilitation need
- Physician referral attached
- Initial PT evaluation attached
- Plan of care attached
- Progress report attached when quantity is greater than 4
